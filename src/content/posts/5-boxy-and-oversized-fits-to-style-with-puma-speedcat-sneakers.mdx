Getting dressed lately feels like solving a puzzle nobody asked for. You want something comfortable, something you can move in without thinking twice, but comfort alone doesn't cut it anymore. Throw on the wrong pieces and suddenly you look like you rolled out of bed. Go too far the other direction and you're overdressed for a coffee run. Sneakers make these balance trickier, especially chunky ones that beg for the right proportions around them. Baggy jeans might swallow them whole. Skinny pants might clash entirely. Finding that middle ground between relaxed and put together takes more thought than most mornings allow.

That's exactly where oversized dressing earns its place. Loose silhouettes and structured sneakers actually complement each other once you know the formula. This roundup breaks down five outfit combinations built specifically around Puma Speedcat sneakers, each one balancing volume, texture, and color without overthinking it. Consider this your shortcut to looking styled, not stressed, every single time you reach for these shoes.

## **Look 01: Camel Coat & Wide-Leg Trousers**

![](/images/content/5-boxy-and-oversized-fits-to-style-with-puma-speedcat-sneakers/pinterest-optimized-1783505891311.webp)

An oversized camel coat sets the mood here, draped loose over the shoulders with a soft, unstructured lapel. Underneath sits a cream turtleknit, fitted enough to balance out all the extra fabric on top. Wide-leg trousers in a matching tan tone keep the palette tonal and calm, pooling gently at the ankle. A slim wristwatch adds a small formal touch without breaking the relaxed mood. Puma Speedcat sneakers finish things off, their low profile grounding the outfit and keeping it from feeling too dressy. The whole combination reads effortless, like something thrown on in minutes but styled with real intention. Neutral tones dominate throughout, proving that monochrome dressing doesn't have to feel boring. This is oversized layering done right, cozy yet still polished enough for daytime errands or a casual lunch out.

## **Look 02: Retro Track Suit & Bold Colorblock**

![](/images/content/5-boxy-and-oversized-fits-to-style-with-puma-speedcat-sneakers/pinterest-optimized-1783505940702.webp)&#x20;

This look leans fully into nostalgia with a boxy zip-up track jacket in navy, cream, and burnt orange. Loose sleeves billow out at the arms, giving off that skater-inspired energy from decades past. Matching track pants continue the colorblock pattern down the leg, wide enough to move freely without looking sloppy. A small orange crossbody bag breaks up the navy tones and adds a pop of contrast against the outfit. Gold hoop earrings bring a touch of shine near the face, simple but noticeable. Sneakers with orange accents tie back to the bag, pulling the whole color story together. Street style photography like this capture's movement and joy, not just clothes on a hanger. Retro sportswear continues making a comeback, and outfits like this show exactly why the trend refuses to fade. Comfort meets attitude in every stitch.

## **Look 03: Chunky Cardigan & Sage Button-Down**

&#x20;![](/images/content/5-boxy-and-oversized-fits-to-style-with-puma-speedcat-sneakers/pinterest-optimized-1783505954240.webp)

Autumn energy fills this outfit through and through, starting with a chunky cream cardigan featuring cable-knit detailing on the sleeves. Underneath, a sage green button-down peek out, buttoned high enough to look put together but casual enough to feel lived-in. Grey pleated trousers add structure below, wide-legged and cropped just above the ankle. A cognac leather tote hangs from one shoulder, its warm tone playing nicely against the muted greens and greys. Layered gold jewelry catches light near the collar without stealing focus from the rest. Sneakers with contrast stripes finish the look, practical for walking cobblestone streets on chilly mornings. Movement defines this photo, caught mid-stride against a backdrop of falling leaves and string lights. Texture does a lot of work here, from cable knit to leather to wool trousers. Fall dressing rarely looks this natural.

## **Look 04: Plaid Flannel Shacket & Baggy Denim**

![](/images/content/5-boxy-and-oversized-fits-to-style-with-puma-speedcat-sneakers/pinterest-optimized-1783505965441.webp)

Cozy meets casual in this shacket look, built around a rust-toned plaid flannel worn loose over a simple white tee. Sleeves stay unbuttoned at the cuff, adding a lived-in feel that suits the whole outfit. Baggy jeans in a washed light blue continue the relaxed silhouette, stacking slightly at the ankle for extra ease. A rust beanie pulls color straight from the flannel, tying the top half together without much effort. Small gold hoops peek out near the ears, subtle against the warm tones surrounding them. A canvas crossbody bag in tan adds function, slung diagonally across the chest for hands-free convenience. Golden hour light wraps around the whole scene, giving everything a soft glow. Sneakers with red accents ground the look in something sporty. Layering like this proves oversized pieces can still feel intentional, never sloppy.

## **Look 05: Grey Trench Coat & Turtleneck Trousers**

![](/images/content/5-boxy-and-oversized-fits-to-style-with-puma-speedcat-sneakers/pinterest-optimized-1783506457387.webp)

Rainy city streets set the scene for this last look, anchored by a long trench coat in warm grey. Belted at the waist but left open here, the coat sways loose with every step, giving off effortless movement. A ribbed turtleneck underneath keeps things cozy, its charcoal shade blending seamlessly into the trousers below. Wide-leg pants continue that same grey family, tailored enough to look sharp despite the relaxed cut through the leg. Black crossbody strap cuts diagonally across the chest, small but noticeable against the lighter coat fabric. White sneakers brighten the bottom of the outfit, a clean contrast against all that muted grey. Wet cobblestones reflect city lights behind her, adding atmosphere without pulling focus from the clothes. Every piece here works in harmony, calm and considered. Rainy day dressing rarely looks this composed.
