That is exactly why this guide exists. These 5 carefully picked back-to-school accessories all come in under $30, and every single one earns its spot in your daily carry. No filler, no overpriced brand names, just smart finds that actually work for student life. Whether you lean minimal or maximalist, this list has something that fits your style and your budget without making you choose between the two.
