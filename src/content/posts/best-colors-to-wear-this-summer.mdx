Best Colors to Wear This Summer

This summer's most popular colors include coral, sky blue, lavender, and mint green.

These shades work well with neutral basics and create fresh seasonal outfits. Bright colors add energy while softer tones offer elegance.

Experiment with color combinations to create unique and stylish looks.
