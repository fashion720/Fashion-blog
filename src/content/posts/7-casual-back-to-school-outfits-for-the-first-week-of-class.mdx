The first week of class always brings the same question: what do you wear? You want to feel comfortable during long lectures, campus walks, and coffee runs, but showing up in old sweats rarely feels right. At the same time, dressing too formally can feel out of place. Finding that sweet spot between relaxed and polished is often harder than expected, especially when mornings get hectic.
That is where this guide comes in. These 7 casual back to school outfits for the first week of class take the stress out of getting dressed. From laid back layers to effortlessly stylish combinations, each look is curated to help you feel confident, comfortable, and ready for everything campus life throws your way. Styling your first week wardrobe just got much easier.

## **Item Name 1: Relaxed Campus Cargo Outfit**

![](/images/content/7-casual-back-to-school-outfits-for-the-first-week-of-class/img%201%20.png)

Walking across campus in a relaxed cargo outfit brings comfort and style to the first week of class. A cozy burgundy cardigan adds warmth during early morning lectures, while the fitted top keeps the look fresh and modern. Soft green cargo pants create an easygoing feel that works well for busy days between classes. Meanwhile, sturdy sneakers provide support for long walks around campus. A crossbody bag decorated with pins adds personality and gives the outfit a fun student vibe. Layered necklaces bring a subtle touch of detail without feeling overdone. Altogether, this casual back to school outfit blends comfort, confidence, and everyday practicality, making it a smart choice for students who want to look stylish while staying comfortable throughout the school day.

## **Item Name 2: Denim Jacket Campus Look**

![](/images/content/7-casual-back-to-school-outfits-for-the-first-week-of-class/img%202.png)

Sitting under the trees between classes, this casual back to school outfit offers a relaxed and stylish feel for the first week of class. An embroidered denim jacket adds character and creates a polished layer for changing campus weather. Light linen pants bring comfort and allow easy movement throughout a busy day. A warm toned top adds a subtle pop of color that feels welcoming and seasonal. Meanwhile, woven slip on shoes keep the look effortless and comfortable for walking around campus. The textured tote bag provides space for books and daily essentials while adding a natural touch. Small accessories and decorative pins give the outfit a personal feel. Overall, this look combines comfort, confidence, and everyday style, making it a great choice for students starting a new semester.

## Item Name 3: Vintage Campus Jacket Outfit

![](/images/content/7-casual-back-to-school-outfits-for-the-first-week-of-class/image%203.png)

Walking across campus in a vintage inspired look brings confidence and comfort to the first week of class. A distressed brown jacket adds texture and gives the outfit a relaxed, timeless feel. Light wash jeans create a casual foundation that works well for lectures, study sessions, and campus activities. Underneath, a soft lace top adds contrast and brings a touch of elegance to the overall look. Brown ankle boots offer comfort for long days while complementing the earthy color palette. A fringe shoulder bag provides enough space for notebooks and daily essentials while adding personality. Statement jewelry completes the outfit with subtle charm. Together, these pieces create a casual back to school outfit that feels stylish, comfortable, and ready for a busy start to the semester.

Whether you're meeting classmates, finding your lecture hall, or grabbing coffee between classes, this back to school outfit delivers comfort, style, and confidence from morning until evening.
